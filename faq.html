Frequently Asked Questions

Rich text sections converted into readable static modules

What Rule Set Do You Use? / How Do I Sign Up For Matches?

Current matches run by CASS use the PCSL rulebook with modified courses of fire to create tactical-feeling stages.

Registration, squadding, and scoring are streamlined through Practiscore. Squad early if you plan on shooting with a group.

What Should I Bring? / What Are Divisions?

For PCSL matches, the minimum equipment is a rifle with sling, a handgun with holster, magazines, and roughly 100 rounds for each firearm. Safety glasses and electronic ear protection are required.

How Are Events Run? / How Should I Show Up?

Show up before the safety briefing with all guns cased and unloaded. Firearms stay cold until instructed to load by a range officer.

What Skill Level Should I Be To Attend A Match?

CASS matches are open to any skill level from beginner to advanced, with an emphasis on safe handling and basic familiarity with your firearms.
