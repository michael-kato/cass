Our mission at Cascade Action Shooting Sports is to empower shooters of all levels of experience to better themselves through both fun and challenging shooting sport events. We strive to provide the shooting community nationwide the opportunity to test their skills and compete with one another through a variety of unique stages and shooting challenges not typically offered elsewhere.

Whether you're an old hand at competition shooting looking to elevate your skills to the next level, a new gun owner looking to build competency and familiarity in a safe, welcoming setting, or just looking to have fun with great shooters, CASS is your gateway to action shooting.
