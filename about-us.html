About Us

Volunteer-run, safety-focused, and built for challenging matches

Cascade Action Shooting Sports is a volunteer-run organization by a group of industry veterans with over a decade of sport shooting experience.

We're dedicated to providing fun and challenging events to the Pacific Northwest region. CASS aims to provide safe, fun and welcoming events to all skill levels.
